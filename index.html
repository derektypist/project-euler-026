Project Euler 026

Reciprocal Cycles

A unit fraction contains 1 in the numerator. The decimal representation of the unit fractions with denominators 2 to 10 are given:
1/2 = 0.5
1/3 = 0.(3)
1/4 = 0.25
1/5 = 0.2
1/6 = 0.1(6)
1/7 = 0.(142857)
1/8 = 0.125
1/9 = 0.(1)
1/10 = 0.1

The aim is to find the value of d less than n, for which 1/d contains the longest recurring cycle in its decimal fraction part. Begin by entering a number.
